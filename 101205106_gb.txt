Student Services and Campus Life

The Student Association of George Brown College (SAGBC), in partnership with the college, provides a variety of student-focused services and resources to support academic success and well-being:

Academic Support: Tutoring, peer mentoring, and academic advising.

Health & Wellness: On-campus Health Centre, counselling services, and student health/dental plans.

Career Services: Career coaching, job search assistance, resume and interview workshops.

Community & Advocacy: Student clubs, social events, Community Care Centre, and the Safewalk Program for student safety.

Nutrition Support: The Student Nutrition Access Program (SNAP) provides free food hampers, on-campus food pantries, and nutritional education resources.
